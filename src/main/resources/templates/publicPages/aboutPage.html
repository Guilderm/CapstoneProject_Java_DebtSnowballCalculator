Topic Project Description:

Snowball Finance is a fictitious financial company created for the final project of the "Desarrollo de Aplicaciones Web y Patrones" Course that provides financial advisory services for debt management and cancellation, based on he "Snowball" methodology. It allows the user, through subscription plans, to obtain tips with experts in the field that will guide the debtor through the best path to get out of their pending accounts.
